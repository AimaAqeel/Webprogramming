CHATHARA CHICKEN BIRYANI

A delicious and flavorful Pakistani rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a blend of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cups basmati rice
  • 250g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/8 cup cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt, to taste

Manner

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for 20 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and ghee. Sauté sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger and garlic paste, green chilies, and chicken pieces to the pot. Cook until chicken is browned.
  4. Stir in yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Layer the soaked rice over the chicken mixture. Sprinkle garam masala, coriander leaves, and mint leaves.
  6. Top with the reserved sautéed onions. Pour water over the rice. Cover and cook until the rice is tender.
  7. Fluff the biryani with a fork, mixing the layers gently. Serve hot with raita or salad.

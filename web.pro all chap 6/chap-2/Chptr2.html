Breaking Bad

Overview:

Breaking Bad is a critically acclaimed television series created by Vince Gilligan. It follows the story of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, as he delves into the criminal underworld to secure his family's financial future after being diagnosed with terminal cancer.

Characters:

Walter White: A high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer.

Jesse Pinkman: A former student of Walter's who becomes his business partner in the drug trade.

Skyler White: Walter's wife who becomes increasingly entangled in his criminal activities.

Hank Schrader: Walter's brother-in-law and a DEA agent determined to bring down the local drug trade.

Saul Goodman: A sleazy lawyer who becomes entangled in Walter and Jesse's affairs.

Gustavo "Gus" Fring: A high-level drug distributor and owner of the Los Pollos Hermanos chain of fast-food chicken restaurants.
